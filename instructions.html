Instructions

Dear participant , thank you for participating! Please read the instructions before the experiment.

Process

The steps for The Experiment are as follows:

  1. A pre-trial will be conducted immediately after you press 'Next'. This is to familiarize you with the copy-paste techniques.
  2. Next, the real experiment will involve 27 trials (copy-paste tasks) per technique. You will be given a one minute break every time you complete the trials for one technique.
  3. Since there are two techniques, there will be 54 trials for one experimental set. You will repeat the set 3 times in total.

What you'll do

The techniques

Traditional

Use CTRL + C, CTRL + V to copy and paste highlighted text.

AutoComPaste

Start by typing the text in the Text Editor window. A dropdown list will appear with possible suggestions.

Pasting text
Use arrow keys ↑ ↓ to select your desired text and press Enter.

Pasting sentences
Use arrow key → to paste the next sentence which comes after the current sentence.
