Instructions

Dear participant , thank you for participating! Please read the instructions before the experiment.

The experiment

In this experiment, we are interested in finding out how AutoComPaste and Ctrl+C, Ctrl+V compare in terms of performance. Hence, we will be asking you to copy and paste text from one window to another.

  • A pre-trial will be conducted immediately after you press 'Next'. This is to familiarize you with the copy-paste techniques.
  • Next, the real experiment will involve 27 trials (copy-paste tasks) per technique. You will be given a one minute break every time you complete the trials for one technique.
  • Since there are two techniques, there will be 54 trials for one experimental set. You will repeat the set 3 times in total.
  • Lastly, you will be invited to fill in a post-experiment questionnaire and we will take any questions you have about the experiment.

What you'll do

The techniques

Traditional

Use CTRL + C, CTRL + V to copy and paste highlighted text.

AutoComPaste

Start by typing the text in the Text Editor window. A dropdown list will appear with possible suggestions. Use your arrow keys ↑ ↓ to select your desired text and press Enter.
